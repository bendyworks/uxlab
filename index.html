About the UX Lab

Our mission is to advance the practice of user experience design and increase design literacy in Madison and surrounding areas by bringing the best minds together to help non-profits, government and civic organizations and students enhance the experience of their products and services. The UXLab is run out of Bendyworks, during time given to us to learn, grow and give back.

WHAT WE DO

USER RESEARCH

User interviews, contextual inquiries and ethnographies, diary studies, card sorts

USABILITY TESTING

Usability testing, task analysis, heuristic analysis, focus groups, feature validation, A/B testing

INNOVATION WORKSHOPS

Mental modeling, collaborative design, prototyping workshops, lectures and talks

How the UX Lab Works

We don't design or develop websites or apps. The UXLab is focused solely on
research and innovation.

Process and Scope

1-hour discovery meeting to learn about problem, history, requirements, etc.

Free tier services will take no longer than 1 work day total (8 hours)

Work will be completed within 1 month of initial meeting.

Summary report available afterwards.

Additional notes, analysis, raw materials, etc. available upon request.

All materials, processes, results, etc. are property of UXLab.

Confidentiality will be assured if any data is released.

All eligible clients get one free service.

Client Responsibilities

Have a well defined project.

Be able to make stakeholders available for meetings and participation.

Help to recruit participants for tests and workshops.

Provide financial incentives for user participation where necessary.
