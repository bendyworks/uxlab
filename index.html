About the UXLab

We help you make digital things that make people happy.

Our mission is to advance the practice of user experience design and increase design literacy in Madison and surrounding areas by bringing the best minds together to help non-profits, government and civic organizations and students enhance the experience of their products and services. The UXLab is run out of Bendyworks, during time given to us to learn, grow and give back.

What We Do

We help you make digital things that make people happy.

User Research

We use user interviews, contextual inquiries, card sorts, data analysis, and other research methods and get information and data you can use to bring your product or service to the next level.

Usability Testing

Have a sketch of an app, or receiving feedback a part of your app or service just isn't working. We'll find target users and run them through the process, and give you a roadmap to help delight your users.

Innovation Consulting

We want to get everyone involved, so we can run collaborative design workshops and brainstorming sessions with stakeholders and users to identify core pain points and future solutions.

The UXLab Does not Currently Provide Interface Design,Visual Design or Development Services. We know people who do though!

For the Best Results

We ask for a little help from you to help the project go smoothly.

  • Have a well defined project.

    Success for research, testing and consulting relies heavily on being able to identify a target audience and establish metrics for success.

  • Make Stakeholders Available

    Having clear vision from stakeholders helps us come up with the most effective plan.

  • Help to recruit participants

    Research requires people who are willing to participate in tests. No one knows the audience better than you.

  • Provide incentives for participation

    We want to compensate people generous enough to participate in research for their time. We provide the elbow grease, you provide the carrot.

Terms and Conditions

Sorry for the small print, but we feel this project will run smoother if everyone is on the same page.

What We Do

The UX Lab at Bendyworks is dedicated to user research and user experience design consulting. We gather data and do quantitative and qualitative research and analysis. We also run workshops and brainstorming sessions. We do not design or develop websites or applications.

Eligibility

We favor non-profits, civic organizations, and government entities. In some cases we will take on student projects if we deem it is within our mission. We will generally not take on for-profit entities, though may in some rare cases.

Financial Support

While our services are and always will be free, providing participation incentives, expenses for software services, and support for incidental costs can help the project be more successful. While financial support is not necessary, we will favor applicants that are willing to provide some financial support. We will always try to keep these costs to a minimum, not to exceed $300 total for a project. All expenses are passed to client at-cost, and will never be marked-up. We will always be 100% transparent regarding expenses.

Selection

Approval of applicants and project engagement is at the sole discretion of the principals of the UX Lab at Bendyworks. We reserve the right to not work with any organization at our sole discretion for any reason we deem appropriate.

We will generally favor engagements with clients that:

  • Have a well defined project and goals
  • Are not-for-profit, government or civic organizations, or students.
  • May help raise the profile of the UX Lab at Bendyworks
  • Have interesting or unique UX design and research challenges
  • Will allow us to use data and work product for marketing and promotional purposes or to aggregate with other data for white papers and blog posts.
  • Are willing to make stakeholders and decision makers heavily involved in the project.
  • Are willing to provide some financial support.
  • Do not have overt political affiliations.
  • Are from Madison, Wisconsin or the surrounding areas.

Ongoing Work

We will not gurantee any additional work or support after our engagement ends.

Capacity

Currently, our capacity is one client per month maximum.

Use of Work Product and Data

By engaging with the UX Lab at Bendyworks, you are allowing us to use any and all data and work product from our engagement for case studies, marketing and promotional purposes, or for use in compendiums, research papers or blog posts. Exceptions can be negotiated, but we will favor applicants that allow us to use work product and data. We will never release or expose sensitive personal information or data that could harm or endanger participants in our research or studies or principals of organizations we engage with.
